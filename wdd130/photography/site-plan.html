Purpose

Has the time come to finally tie the knot? Is graduation just around the corner? Are those babies the cutest! We all want to capture the most significant times in our lives. I have the best opportunity for you. With this website, you will be able see what this photographer can do, and you will know with a certainty that you want your pictures taken from her. There will be many examples for you to see and you will see what prices may be right for you. So, don't be shy and give us a try.

Audience

This website is for everyone that is looking for a good photographer to take their photos. It doesn't matter what the circumstances may be, the photographer can do them all. A graduate needs to take photos for his grad-announcement. Marriages are a big event in life and a photographer is the right person for the job. If there is a new baby coming soon, then someone is going to need to find the right person to take their photos.
